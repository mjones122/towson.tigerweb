Surveillance & AI

Monitoring in real time and recognizing threat:  AI-enabled video security cameras have the  ability to recognize things and people in the field of view and produce alerts or warning signals.

Reliability in identifying invasions:  AI camera systems have the ability to detect motion,      which allows them to separate between the movements of people and animals.

Smart metal detection: Driven by AI security cameras are better and thorough at detecting  all weapons and other dangerous objects.

 Protecting retail spaces from theft and losses: Some artificial intelligence systems are made to protect stores from theft or loss of property. This technology is able to recognize questionable activity and sound an alarm.

Improved data recognition in an a crisis: driven by AI camera systems quicken and simplify the data gathering process.

Face recognition for people tracking: AI cameras are capable of recognizing faces, which makes it simple to identify people.
