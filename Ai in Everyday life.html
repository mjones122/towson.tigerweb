AI in Everyday Life

 Business: By making product recommendations based on users' search histories and browsing habits, artificial intelligence can increase sales for businesses.

Education:  By reviewing past exams and learning records, AI can identify a student's areas of weakness and create specific learning objectives. AI maximizes student learning.

 HealthCare: Artificial Intelligence is a powerful tool that is assisting medical professionals in their daily discoveries, but it will never be able to fully replace the steadiness and efficiency of a human hand.
